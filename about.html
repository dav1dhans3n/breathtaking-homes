Established in 2016

Brad's path to becoming an entrepreneur started with small to large home improvement projects at home while growing up. It seemed like his parents were always working on the family home and Brad was always involved.

From the start he always loved working with his hands and building anything he could. He learned about doing things the right way vs. doing things the cheap way early.

After high school he went to Del Tech and earned a degree in entrepreneurship and was eager to start his career by starting Breathtaking Homes LLC.

Who we are...

In 2016, Brad Hansen began Breathtaking Homes with small projects as he learned to run a business. The projects grew, his team grew, his business grew, then his family grew as he married the love of his life Stephanie in 2019. And now their family has grown with the addition of Gracie in 2021!
